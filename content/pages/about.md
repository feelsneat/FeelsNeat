FeelsNeat exists because we enjoy the feeling of tidy, well-organized digital environments. 

We don't build to follow temporary trends or promote complex frameworks. We build because we believe that digital experiences are better when they are simple and well-crafted. Everything we release—whether it is an internal developer tool, a small open-source package, a detailed observation article, or a physical resource—is designed to feel neat.
