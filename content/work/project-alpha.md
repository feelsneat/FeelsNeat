AeroSpace Tech creates complex software for satellite tracking. While their technical engine was highly capable, their marketplace listings and product mockups felt outdated, making it hard to build trust with new customers.

We redesigned their visual presentation layout:
1. **Mockup Design**: Cleaned up the hardware frames, focusing strictly on the charts and visual data.
2. **Contrast & Colors**: Adjusted the saturation to make text crisp and legible.
3. **Marketing Graphic Assets**: Created high-quality presentation graphics showing key UI interactions.
